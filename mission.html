Mission

Agency should not be a luxury. The ability to act with clarity, free from manipulation, informed by intelligence that works for you — this should be available to everyone.

Syntra is working toward a future where personal AI agents are as common and accessible as web browsers. Not locked behind subscriptions or cloud infrastructure, but running locally on the devices people already own.

Edge-first agents

AI that depends on distant servers is AI that can be taken away. We're exploring how to build agents that run fast on consumer hardware — phones, laptops, single-board computers — with no cloud dependency. Local means private, fast, and always available.

Manipulation defense

The internet is full of systems designed to exploit human psychology — dark patterns, deceptive design, engagement traps, and persuasion techniques engineered to override your judgment. Your agent should recognize these patterns and shield you from them before they take effect.

Proactive, not intrusive

The best assistant speaks up when it matters and stays quiet when it doesn't. We're interested in agents that surface useful guidance at the right moment — not ones that interrupt, nag, or overwhelm you with irrelevant suggestions.

Interpretable and modifiable

An agent you can't understand is an agent you can't trust. We believe agents should be interpretable enough that users can make meaningful modifications to suit their individual needs — without transgressing the wholeness of others. This means transparent reasoning, readable structure, and composable parts that invite ownership rather than dependence.

What's next

This is the starting point. The problems are deep and the solutions are evolving. We'll be sharing research, prototypes, and references to others doing important work in this space.
